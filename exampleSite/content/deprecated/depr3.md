#### Legacy `Webfuse` capitalisation in widget snippet

The `Webfuse` (capital W) identifier in the widget script snippet is deprecated. Use `webfuse` (lowercase) instead. The old form will continue to work for the foreseeable future but may be removed in a future major release.

- Change `new Webfuse(...)` to `new webfuse(...)` in your embed snippets
